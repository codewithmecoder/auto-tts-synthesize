PAXINE HAS A GIFT. On her twelfth birthday, her grandma gives her an exotic spotted cat named Tache. As soon as their eyes meet, Paxine finds she can see and hear through Tache’s eyes and ears. She thinks Tache is special, but her grandma says it’s the other way around. However, when she gets thrown into the adult world of politics and murder, she finds out how special Tache is. But can he protect her? He is just a kitten after all. Isn’t he?
